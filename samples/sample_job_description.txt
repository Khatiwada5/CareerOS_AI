Data Analyst Intern - Northstar Analytics

Northstar Analytics is seeking a Data Analyst Intern to help clean, analyze, and visualize customer and product data. The intern will work with business stakeholders to understand questions, write SQL queries, build Python/Pandas analyses, and create dashboards for weekly reporting.

Responsibilities:
- Clean and analyze datasets using SQL, Python, and Excel.
- Build dashboards and summaries for product and operations teams.
- Communicate findings clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Document assumptions, methods, and recommendations.

Qualifications:
- Current college student or recent graduate in computer science, business analytics, statistics, economics, or related field.
- Experience with SQL, Python, Pandas, Excel, Tableau or Power BI.
- Strong communication, curiosity, and attention to detail.
- Must be authorized to work in the United States. Sponsorship is not available for this internship.
